EduGrantHub

EduGrantHub is an online platform designed to facilitate the application, management, and disbursement of scholarships. It serves as a centralized hub where students can search for scholarships, check eligibility, submit applications, upload required documents, and track their application status. Additionally, it helps scholarship providers manage the selection process, communicate with applicants, and distribute funds efficiently.
